The Science of MedFuse

Bridging the gap between structural anatomy and functional metabolism through advanced artificial intelligence.

Multimodal Neural Fusion

MedFuse utilizes a sophisticated Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) architecture. It extracts high-frequency structural details from MRI scans and merges them flawlessly with the metabolic intensity heatmaps of PET scans, ensuring zero signal degradation.

Automated Anomaly Targeting

Say goodbye to manual tracing. Our physical anomaly detection system is fully automated. The model instantly identifies areas of hyper-metabolic radiotracer uptake and generates precise bounding boxes to highlight points of interest.

Generative AI Reporting

Powered by Google's Gemini systems, MedFuse rapidly interprets complex numerical metrics and distributions to generate dynamic, clinical-grade preliminary reports in seconds, drastically reducing radiologist workload.

Clinical Impact & Vision

Our core mission is to democratize access to advanced computational diagnostic tools. By eliminating the necessity for incredibly expensive, specialized scanning hardware to perform multimodal fusion, MedFuse empowers hospitals and clinics across the globe to perform high-tier analysis using standard, accessible computing resources.

Accelerated Triage

Drastically reduces the time taken to identify life-threatening anomalies by automatically highlighting crucial regions of interest, allowing emergency trauma cases to be prioritized and treated instantly.

Reduced Human Error

By cleanly overlaying functional hotspot data directly onto structural anatomy, physicians no longer need to mentally map and compare separate scans, significantly lowering the risk of cognitive fatigue and diagnostic oversight.

Global Accessibility

Operates smoothly via a secure, cloud-based web interface, meaning zero dependency on local on-premise supercomputers, expensive software licenses, or complicated IT deployments.
